Our Story

Crafting luxury candles inspired by the mystical phases of the moon

How Lunar Essence Was Born

In the heart of Jamaica, where the Caribbean moon casts its enchanting glow over lush landscapes, Lunar Essence was born from a passion for creating extraordinary sensory experiences. Our founder discovered the ancient art of candle making during a transformative journey through the island's mystical nights, where each lunar phase seemed to whisper secrets of tranquility and renewal.

What began as a personal quest for the perfect ambiance evolved into a mission to share the magic of hand-poured luxury with the world. Every candle we create is infused with the spirit of Jamaica's natural beauty and the timeless wisdom of lunar cycles.

Today, Lunar Essence stands as a testament to artisanal craftsmanship, where each candle is carefully hand-poured with premium natural soy wax, wooden wicks, and thoughtfully curated fragrances that transport you to a realm of celestial serenity.

Our Mission

To create premium, hand-poured candles inspired by the mystical phases of the moon, bringing luxury, tranquility, and celestial elegance into every home.

Our Vision

To become the premier destination for luxury candles that blend artisanal craftsmanship with celestial inspiration, creating unforgettable sensory experiences for our customers worldwide.
